/Get to know us

A Tropical Retreat

We believe in the power of nature and the serenity it brings. Our retreat is set in the heart of Sri Lanka's tropical paradise. Whether you seek adventure or profound relaxation, our retreat offers the perfect blend of both.

Yoga, Surfing, and Meditation

Immerse yourself in the art of yoga and meditation with our experienced instructors. Begin your day with sunrise yoga on the beach, where the gentle waves and golden sun create an unparalleled ambiance. For the adventurous spirit, ride the waves of Hikkaduwa's famous surf spots, and let the sea energize your soul.

Delightful Dining

Our open-air restaurant not only offers delectable Sri Lankan and international cuisine but also a dining experience like no other. Enjoy your meals with a backdrop of endless rice fields, where the vibrant greenery meets the clear blue sky. Our menu is curated to reflect the flavors of the island, using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients that will tantalize your taste buds.

Join Yoga Retreat

For those in Europe seeking an unforgettable getaway, we specialize in organizing group retreats tailored to your preferences. Whether it's a yoga and meditation retreat, a surfing adventure, or simply a serene escape from the hustle and bustle, we have the experience and expertise to make your trip seamless and memorable.
