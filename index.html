Welcome in Ceylon's House

Discover our family hotel nestled in front of a magnificent jungle and panoramic rice field view. Here, you can find peace of mind and relaxation while experiencing Sri Lankan hospitality with a touch of European style, offering you the best of both worlds.

We have created nine rooms, a rooftop restaurant, and a pool for you to enjoy on those hot days. But that's not all. Explore our special offerings such as our Surfing School, massage and Ayurveda therapy, tailored trips, and unique, incredibly fresh seafood.

Enjoy rooms with a jungle view and balcony

Our boho rooms with private balconies offer the perfect retreat for relaxation and rejuvenation. Each room features modern amenities, comfortable furnishings, and stunning views of the surrounding jungle and rice fields. Wake up to the sound of nature and enjoy your morning coffee on your private balcony.

Experience a restaurant in the middle of the jungle

Welcome to our Rooftop Restaurant, where culinary excellence meets breathtaking views. Perched atop our hotel, our restaurant offers a unique dining experience that combines the flavors of Sri Lanka with a touch of European finesse.
